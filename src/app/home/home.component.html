“And let us consider how to stir up one another to love and good works, not neglecting to meet together, as is the habit of some, but encouraging one another, and all the more as you see the Day drawing near.”

~Hebrews 10:24-25~

Powerful Search

Our application includes a powerful search function that not only lets users find Bible verses quickly but can also find related verses thanks to machine learning. A search function is a powerful tool for user’s private and group studies. Users can see commentaries posted by other users of the application to further their understanding of a verse or passage.

Shared Bible

The Shared Bible helps keep Bible study members connected even when they aren't together. Users will be able to share their notes on any passage with their group allowing them to continue discussions from their study going throughout the week. Have a question for your group? With the Shared Bible’s question proposal system you won't have to wait until the next time your study group meets to ask it.

Elegant Dashboard

Each user will have their own personal, customizable dashboard that will allow them to stay up to date on announcements, messages, questions, and notes made by their fellow members. The dashboard is a custom tailored experience, honed to make everything as elegant and straightforward to comprehend and to get information quickly and efficiently.
